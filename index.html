// research & projects

Featured work

TARGET-NBL-survival-analysis Active

Survival analysis and exploratory data analysis on the TARGET Neuroblastoma (TARGET-NBL) paediatric cancer dataset. Focused on identifying clinical risk factors and stratifying patients by outcome probability.

  • Kaplan–Meier survival curves with log-rank testing
  • Clinical feature analysis and risk stratification
  • Reproducible pipeline from public NCI TARGET data
Python pandas lifelines matplotlib seaborn
In active development: Breast cancer RNA-seq (GSE176078, DESeq2) · TCGA-LUAD biomarker study · and more incoming.
